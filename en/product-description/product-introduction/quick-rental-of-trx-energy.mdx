# When Should You Use Quick Rent?

Quick Rent is designed for **temporary, occasional, low-frequency, one-off** needs‚Äîor whenever you **don‚Äôt want to freeze TRX**.

Here are a few scenarios to help you decide when Quick Rent is the best fit.

---

**Example 1: Occasional USDT (TRC20) Transfers Without Energy**

The user rarely sends transactions on TRON.  
When they need to transfer USDT, the address lacks Energy and the network will burn a significant amount of TRX as fees.

üëâ Use **Quick Rent** to purchase short-term Energy (for example, 30 minutes or 1 hour) and complete the transfer at a much lower cost.

---

**Example 2: Temporary DApp Contract Interaction**

The user occasionally opens a DApp to stake, swap, or craft assets.  
Without Energy, the transaction cannot be executed.

üëâ Use **Quick Rent** to replenish Energy, then run the contract call immediately.

---

**Example 3: ‚ÄúActivating‚Äù a New Wallet**

New addresses usually have zero Bandwidth/Energy, so even basic TRX transfers may fail.

üëâ Use **Quick Rent** to add the basic resources and make the address usable right away.

---

**Example 4: Short-Term Batch Transfers (Airdrops / Rewards / Rebates)**

Merchants, agents, or project teams occasionally need to execute a large number of transfers in a short window.

üëâ Lease a large amount of Energy for a short period via Quick Rent,  
then batch-complete the airdrops or transfers without paying high TRX fees.

---

**Summary**

**If you rarely use TRON but must perform a transaction today, Quick Rent is for you.**

# Quick Rent Step-by-Step Guide

### **Step 1: Connect Your Wallet**

1. Open the Quick Rent page  
2. Click **‚ÄúConnect Wallet‚Äù** (currently supports TronLink)
![Connect Wallet](/en/images/product-description/LinkWallet.png)
After connecting, the system reads your resource status (whether Bandwidth/Energy is sufficient).

> **Why connect a wallet?**  
> GasStation needs to delegate the leased resources to your address so you can use them instantly.

### **Step 2: Choose the Amount of Energy You Need**

![Choose Energy](/en/images/product-description/quick-rental-1.png)
Enter the amount of Energy you want to lease in ‚ÄúResource Amount,‚Äù or simply input the expected number of transactions. The system will automatically recommend an Energy value.

- **1 USDT (TRC20) transfer**  
  ‚ö° Requires roughly 15,000 ‚Äì 30,000 Energy

> **Why estimate the count?**  
> Each transfer or contract call consumes Energy. Renting enough in a single order prevents repeated purchases and avoids transaction failures.

### **Step 3: Select the Leasing Duration (Critical)**
![Select Duration](/en/images/product-description/quick-rental-2.png)

- **One-time transfer ‚Üí 10‚Äì30 minutes is enough**  
- **Multiple operations expected ‚Üí 1 hour is safer**  
- **DApp interactions over a longer period ‚Üí Consider 3 hours or more**

> **Why does duration matter?**  
> The leased resources remain valid only within the rent period.  
> The longer the duration, the longer your resources stay available‚Äîso you can complete transactions without timing out.

> ‚ö† **Important:**
