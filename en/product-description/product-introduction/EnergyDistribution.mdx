## Energy Resale Service Overview

**GasStation.ai Energy Resale Service** provides energy resale capabilities for ecosystem partners, helping you quickly build your own TRON energy distribution and service network with a lower integration threshold.  
Through standardized strategy configuration and automated fulfillment mechanisms, you can complete payment collection, energy distribution, order tracking, and revenue management without any development investment, improving resource operation efficiency and business conversion capability.

![quick-rental-2.png](/images/product-description/fenxiao001.png)

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. User Payment Triggers a Service Request">
    After the end user transfers funds to your preset receiving address, the system will automatically identify the payment, match it with the corresponding resale strategy, and trigger the subsequent service process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. The Platform Automatically Completes Fulfillment">
    Once the payment is received, GasStation.ai will automatically execute the service flow based on your configured strategy, including cost deduction, energy distribution, and order creation, without any manual intervention.
  </Step>
  <Step title="3. A Complete Business Loop Is Formed">
    The user completes the payment, the platform completes fulfillment, and you earn the corresponding resale profit based on your preset pricing strategy. The entire process includes clear fund flow logic and traceable order records, making later operations and management more efficient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

<Note>
**Cost and Profit Example**

**1. User Payment Triggers Demand**  
After your user transfers TRX to your preset receiving address (for example, **5 TRX**), the system identifies the transaction as an energy purchase request and processes it according to the corresponding strategy.

**2. The System Automatically Deducts Fulfillment Costs and Distributes Energy**  
Once the payment is received, the system will automatically complete the following actions:
- Deduct the actual fulfillment cost from your GasStation.ai account balance (for example, **2 TRX**)
- Instantly distribute the corresponding amount of energy to the user’s address (for example, **64,000 energy**)
- No manual intervention is required throughout the entire process

**3. You Earn the Price Difference as Profit**  
Based on this example:
- User payment: **5 TRX**
- Actual cost: **2 TRX**
- **Your profit: 3 TRX**

Profits are settled automatically, with clear and transparent logic.
</Note>

## Step-by-Step Guide
Before configuration, you need to connect your wallet and enable the Energy Resale feature. You can enter the Energy Resale module through either of the following methods:

    - **Link Entry**: Click the entry button on the Energy Resale landing page
    ![Step 1](/images/product-description/fenxiao003.png)
    - **Personal Center**: Go to the "Energy Resale" module from your Personal Center
    ![Step 1.1](/images/product-description/fenxiao004.png)

    <Tip>Before your first use, please make sure your wallet is connected. TronLink and OKX Wallet are currently supported, with TronLink set as the default priority.</Tip>

    
  
<Steps>
  

  <Step title="Create a Resale Strategy">
  After clicking "Add Package", select the corresponding pricing method:
  ![Step 2](/images/product-description/fenxiao005.png)
    When creating a resale strategy, you must first determine the pricing mechanism. The pricing method will directly affect order fulfillment costs, profit structure, and overall risk control.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Dynamic Pricing (Recommended)" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
        Set a markup ratio based on real-time costs, and the system will automatically adjust the selling price to reduce the risk of resource price fluctuations.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Fixed Pricing" icon="tag">
        Set a fixed selling price for each package, suitable for scenarios where you have a clear judgment of market costs or want to maintain stable pricing.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    ![Pricing Options](/images/product-description/fenxiao006.png)

    <Info>
      **Strategy Suggestion**：
      If your goal is to build a long-term and stable resale business, **dynamic pricing** is recommended as the default choice; fixed pricing is more suitable for strategy-driven operations.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Resale Packages">
    Complete the detailed rental duration and payment configuration based on the selected pricing model (dynamic or fixed).

    **Configuration Under Dynamic Pricing**
    ![Dynamic Config](/images/product-description/fenxiao008.png)
    - **Enable Packages**: "10 minutes (10m)" is enabled by default, and can be extended to 1h/1d.
    - **Receiving Address**: It is recommended that each package use an independent TRON address to avoid order identification issues.
    - **Pricing Ratio**: Set a markup ratio (such as 120%), and the system will automatically calculate the selling price using `Selling Price = Cost × Pricing Ratio`.

    **Configuration Under Fixed Pricing**
    ![Fixed Config](/images/product-description/fenxiao009.png)
    - Manually enter the selling price for each order under "Fixed Pricing" (unit: TRX).
    <Warning>
      **Cost Risk Warning**: Fixed pricing does not automatically adjust with market cost changes. If the cost rises above the selling price, the order will still be fulfilled normally, but the difference will be deducted from your account balance, which may result in a loss.
    </Warning>

    <Tip>All modes support switching the unit display between **SUN / TRX** in the resource display area. Before execution, please make sure to check and agree to the relevant agreement, and ensure that the receiving address is valid and used only for the current strategy.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link and Brand Configuration">
    Customize your exclusive resale page to improve brand recognition and optimize user conversion.

    ![Link Config](/images/product-description/fenxiao0010.png)

    - **Link Permission**: The platform provides 1 free link creation quota (contact customer service if you need more).
    - **Multilingual Support**: Independent Chinese and English configuration is supported, and the system will automatically display content based on the user's language preference.
    - **Custom Content**: Includes website name, slogan, contact information, and brand image/logo.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share and Promote">
    After completing the configuration, you can generate your exclusive channel and start business operations.

    ![Share](/images/product-description/fenxiao0011.png)

    - **Exclusive Link**: Copy the exclusive promotional link generated by the system with one click.
    - **Promotion Tools**: You can use the platform’s preset promotional copy, or click "Edit" at any time to modify and adjust the current package.
  </Step>
</Steps>
